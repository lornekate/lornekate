From Madrid

Train

Approximately 4 hours (Madrid Atocha → Ronda)

Link for train tickets can be found here

There are 2 direct trains per day

Recommended schedule for optimal arrival time: 3:05 PM - 6:57 PM

Cost: €47 (Standard)/person or €56 (Standard Plus)/person

Please note that currently the earliest arrival on Sundays in Ronda from Madrid is in the evening. You may want to consider arriving in Ronda on Saturday so that you can attend the Sunday evening Welcome Dinner.
