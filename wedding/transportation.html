Transportation

Listed below is the current information about some suggested transit routes for transportation to get to Ronda. These schedules and prices are based on information currently available and are subject to change. You may want to consider arriving in Ronda on Saturday so that you can attend the Sunday evening Welcome Dinner.

From Madrid

Train

Approximately 4 hours

Madrid Atocha → Ronda

There is 1 direct train to Ronda per day and it departs at 3:05 PM.

Ticket prices vary, starting at $71 CAD + $9.95 CAD Booking Fee
